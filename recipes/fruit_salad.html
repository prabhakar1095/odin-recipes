Fruit Salad recipe

Description

This fruit salad is a colorful mix of fresh, seasonal fruits, lightly sweetened with honey and brightened with lemon juice. It's a healthy, refreshing snack or dessert that’s quick to prepare. Each bite offers a delightful contrast of textures—from crisp apples and juicy grapes to soft bananas and zesty citrus. Garnished with mint leaves, it’s not only vibrant in appearance but also packed with vitamins, perfect for breakfast, a snack, or a light, guilt-free treat.

Ingredients

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Orange
  • Grapes
  • Pineapple
  • Pomegranate seeds
  • Lemon or lime juice
  • Honey
  • Black salt or chaat masala
  • Mint leaves

Steps

  1. Wash fruits:

    Rinse apples, grapes, and oranges under running water to remove dirt and pesticides.

  2. Chop ingredients:

    Cut apples, bananas, and pineapple into bite-sized pieces. Peel orange segments carefully.

  3. Mix & season:

    Combine all fruits in a bowl. Add lemon juice to prevent browning and drizzle honey for sweetness.

  4. Garnish & serve:

    Top with mint leaves or nuts. Chill for 15 minutes before serving for best taste.
