Pizza recipe

Description

Pizza is a popular and versatile dish that originated in Italy. It consists of a soft, chewy dough base topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings such as vegetables, meats, or herbs. This homemade pizza recipe is easy to follow and allows you to customize flavors to your liking. Perfect for family meals, parties, or a quick weekend treat, it combines a crispy crust with gooey, melted cheese and savory toppings a deliciously satisfying experience.

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 packet (7 g) instant yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water

For the sauce:

  • ½ cup tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • ½ tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste

For the toppings:

  • 1–2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced vegetables (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.)
  • Optional: pepperoni, cooked chicken, or other meats
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

1. Prepare the dough:

  1. Mix warm water, sugar, and yeast in a bowl. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough for 5–10 minutes until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough and let it rise for 1–2 hours, or until it doubles in size.

2. Prepare the sauce:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5–10 minutes.

3. Assemble the pizza:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled dough on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your desired toppings.

4. Bake:

  1. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.

5. Serve:

  1. Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, and garnish with fresh basil if desired. Slice and enjoy!
